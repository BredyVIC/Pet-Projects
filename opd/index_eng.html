About us
We are a creative group of students of Novosibirsk State Technical University! Our passion for animal care it has become an integral part of our lives. That's why we decided to create our own virtual route, offering a unique brochure with a detailed action plan for those who want to become a volunteer in the animal world!

Shelters in Novosibirsk
Preliminary trip plan:
Purpose of the trip: Help the pet shelter by providing them with the necessary help and care.
Collect information and photographs to draw attention to the work of the shelter and the problems of homeless animals.
**Decide on the date and place:**
**Action plan:**
1. **Preparing for the trip**
- Collection of necessary materials and resources (food, clothing, medicines for animals).
- Definition of the team of participants and their responsibilities.
2. **Arrival at the shelter**
- Arrival at the shelter, greeting of the shelter staff.
- Inspection of the shelter and organization of workplaces.
- Meeting with animals and starting caring for them.
3. **Work in a shelter**
- taking care of animals.(Feeding, cleaning, walking, etc.)
- Collecting information and photos for a future report and stories about the work of the shelter.
4. **Completion of the trip**
- Summing up the results of the trip.
- Report and processing of the collected material (photos, videos)
**Monitoring and evaluation:**
- During the trip, keep a daily log of events and record important moments.
- Collect feedback and impressions of participants and staff of the shelter.
